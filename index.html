We started out as tadpoles struggling to swim through 102 and 131, and were lucky enough to make it out alive and call ourselves true graphic design majors. After a grueling first year application process, followed by three years of back-breaking hard work, we’ve finally made it to the end. As a class, we’ve been through cube projects, type plates, and Pantone lectures...just to name a few. We’ve pulled all-nighters, made the COD our second home, and sailed through adventures in Vendoland. Our four years of undergraduate study come to a conclusion in an Open Ending, with each of us going our own separate ways.

Go ahead. Check us out.
